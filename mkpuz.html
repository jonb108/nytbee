Step 1 Pangramic Word

Enter a pangramic word:

In this context a pangramic word is one that has exactly 7 unique letters.

Here are three lists of pangramic words:

  1. From the NYT Puzzles
  2. From a list of 10,000 commonly used words (from first20hours github repo)
  3. From a large lexicon (some rare and esoteric - from /usr/share/dict/words on OSX)
Note that words in list 1 do not appear in list 2. The lists are disjoint. You can also just enter 7 different letters and see if there are any words in the lists that use those 7. Try, for example, abclrne
