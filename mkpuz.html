Step 1 Pangramic Word

Enter a pangramic word:

In this context a pangramic word is one that has exactly 7 unique letters.

Here are some lists of pangramic words:

1. From the NYT Puzzles

2. From a list of 10,000 commonly used words (from englishgrammarhere.com)

3. From a large lexicon (some rare and esoteric - from /usr/share/dict/words on OSX)

Note that words in list 1 do not appear in list 2. The lists are disjoint. You can also just enter 7 different letters and see if there are any words in a dictionary that use them all. Try, for example, abclrne
